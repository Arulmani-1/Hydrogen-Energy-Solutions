References & current projects

IN PLANNING: 75MW HYDROGEN FACTORY IN GREECE

Hydrogen Energy Solutions is currently planning to build a 75MW hydrogen plant on an approximately 100ha plot of land in Greece. We will export the hydrogen by ship and unload it in an existing pipeline in the port. 20% of production is oxygen, which we will supply to surrounding sewage treatment plants and hospitals. The system is supplied with energy via wind power and photovoltaics, which we will also install here. With direct access to the sea, the water required is treated via a desalination plant.

IN PLANNING: 25MW PLANT IN BULGARIA

Hydrogen Energy Solutions is currently planning to build a 25 MW hydrogen plant with a 50 MW photovoltaic system in Bulgaria, near Sofia. We export the green hydrogen directly to our contractual partner through the railway network connected to the property. This project serves as a flagship project in Bulgaria and has already been approved by the EU.

IN PLANNING: 50MW SYSTEM IN GERMANY

Hydrogen Energy Solutions is currently planning to build 50 MW hydrogen production in connection with a 210 MW photovoltaic system on a former power plant site in Veltheim. Here, in collaboration with a data center, we plan to convert the data center's water used for cooling into hydrogen and supply it to surrounding customers via the existing railway connection on the property.

IN PLANNING: 150 MW PLANT IN BULGARIA

With our affiliated partners in Bulgaria, we are in the approval process for the construction of a 150 MW photovoltaic system with direct feed into the public grid.
