Powering long-haul trucking fleets with zero-emission fuel cells, offering faster refueling and greater range than battery alternatives.

Liquid hydrogen and green ammonia solutions for international shipping, eliminating sulfur and carbon emissions.

Developing lightweight cryogenic tanks and synthetic Sustainable Aviation Fuels (SAF) derived from green hydrogen.
