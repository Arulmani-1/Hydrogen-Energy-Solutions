Hydrogen Energy Solutions

Hydrogen Energy-Solutions GmbH is a leading expert and project developer in the field of hydrogen technology. We develop innovative concepts and self-sufficient island solutions for the production of green hydrogen in combination with the latest photovoltaic and wind power technology.

Our technologies are modular and scalable and can also be integrated into waste heat streams from upstream or downstream processes. We implement and optimize tailor-made technologies for sustainable and climate-neutral hydrogen production.

Be it PEM, alkaline, AEM, solid oxide or capillary electrolysis — we create hydrogen systems that meet your requirements. This also includes the CCS process for reducing CO2 emissions. Our production of ammonia or methanol also simplifies the supply chain for larger hydrogen production.

We have also developed special energy concepts for residential and commercial properties.

With our solutions you can break away from public utilities and regulate your energy supply independently.

HYDROGEN STORAGE

Our hydrogen storage offers you the highest quality and safety. We provide energy storage systems for efficient storage in various formats, including cylinder bundles up to 700 bar, 40-foot iron oxide containers, battery systems and compressed air storage up to 50 MW.

Our hydrogen storage systems are not only safe and efficient, but also environmentally friendly. The use of hydrogen as an energy source is an important measure in the fight against climate change. With our storage systems, we help ensure that renewable energy sources such as wind and solar energy can be used even better.

We also offer you first-class service: from advice and planning to installation and maintenance of your system, we are at your side with our know-how. We attach great importance to your individual needs and take these into account in every phase of the project.

HYDROGEN DELIVERY SYSTEMS

State-of-the-art storage without environmentally hazardous materials is used either in bundles of bottles up to 700 bar or in non-explosive containers. Our delivery systems store hydrogen in solid materials, which means the storage capacity is 10 times higher than lithium batteries and the hydrogen is not classified as dangerous goods. We attach great importance to the fact that our materials are recyclable and organic in nature.

Through our innovative storage solutions, we actively contribute to environmental protection and offer our customers a sustainable alternative to conventional energy storage. Our technology makes it possible to store hydrogen safely and efficiently without having to use environmentally hazardous materials.

We are aware that the demand for clean energy sources continues to increase and we are proud to be able to contribute to achieving these goals. We are committed to ensuring that our delivery system is not only ecologically compatible, but also makes economic sense for our customers.

MANUFACTURING

In our highly innovative hydrogen energy factories, we produce not only high-quality hydrogen but also oxygen according to pharmaceutical guidelines. Producing abroad allows us to have 30% lower production, maintenance and energy costs. Hydrogen Energy Solutions is therefore able to beat the lowest market prices, even when delivering to Germany.

STORAGE

We generate electricity from renewable energies such as wind power, photovoltaics and water. The hydrogen obtained from this is compressed into special storage units without any energy loss in order to integrate it across industries. We are convinced: The innovative solutions from Hydrogen Energy Solutions will become crucial building blocks for a safe, clean and affordable energy supply and mobility of the future.

DELIVERY & TRANSPORT

Hydrogen Energy Solutions delivers net zero across industries. The green hydrogen is delivered in specially developed, explosion-proof containers. Within the EU, the hydrogen is transported by ship at a pressure of 500 bar. It is unloaded in EU ports and either fed into existing pipelines or delivered to customers by truck.
