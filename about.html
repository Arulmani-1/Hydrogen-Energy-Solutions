Our Story

Leading the Global Transition

Founded by visionaries in the energy sector, Hydrogen Energy Solutions has grown from a pioneering startup into a Fortune 500 powerhouse. We recognized early that green hydrogen is the missing link in the clean energy transition.

Today, we operate the world's largest electrolysis plants and most extensive hydrogen distribution networks, serving industries ranging from heavy manufacturing to global shipping.
